About Claude Builder Club

Empowering University of Rwanda students to shape the future with AI

Our Story

Claude Builder Club started in 2024 when a group of curious students at University of Rwanda came together with a shared vision: making AI accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical background.

We noticed that while AI was transforming industries worldwide, many students felt intimidated by the technology or didn't know where to start. We believed that hands-on learning and community support could change that.

Today, we're a thriving community of learners, builders, and innovators. From computer science majors to business students, from complete beginners to experienced coders - everyone has a place here. What unites us is curiosity and the desire to build meaningful solutions for Rwanda and beyond.

💡

Our Belief

"The best way to learn AI is to build with it. Everyone can be a builder - you just need to start somewhere."

Mission & Objectives

What drives us forward every day

Our Mission

To democratize AI literacy at University of Rwanda by providing hands-on learning experiences that empower students from all disciplines to build practical solutions using Claude AI and related technologies.

Our Vision

A future where every Rwandan student has the skills and confidence to leverage AI as a tool for innovation, problem-solving, and positive impact in their communities and careers.

Key Objectives

🎓

Educate

🔧

🤝

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

🙌 Inclusivity

Everyone belongs here. We welcome students from all backgrounds, disciplines, and skill levels. No question is too basic, no idea is too ambitious.

💪 Learning by Doing

Theory is important, but building is better. We prioritize hands-on projects and practical applications over passive learning.

🌐 Local Impact

We focus on building solutions relevant to Rwanda and Africa. Technology should serve our communities and address real challenges.

📢 Open Knowledge

We share everything we learn. Our resources, projects, and insights are freely available to help others on their journey.

🌱 Growth Mindset

Mistakes are learning opportunities. We celebrate experimentation and continuous improvement over perfection.

⚖️ Responsible AI

We're committed to building AI applications that are ethical, safe, and beneficial. We think critically about the impact of technology.
