Membership Details

Everything you need to know about joining

✅ Who Can Join?

  • Any University of Rwanda student (all campuses)
  • Students from all faculties and disciplines
  • All skill levels - complete beginners welcome!
  • No programming experience required
  • Alumni and staff may join as associate members

💰 Membership Fee

FREE

That's right - membership is completely free! We're funded through partnerships and university support.

📅 Meeting Schedule

  • Weekly Workshops
    Every Saturday, 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
    CST Building, Room 201
  • Monthly Meetups
    Last Saturday of each month, 4:00 PM
    Campus Cafeteria
  • Office Hours
    Tuesdays & Thursdays, 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM
    CST Building, Tech Hub

📝 What to Expect

As a member, you'll:

  1. Week 1: Welcome orientation, introduction to Claude AI, join WhatsApp group
  2. Weeks 2-4: Attend beginner workshops, complete introductory tutorials
  3. Month 2: Start your first mini-project with mentor support
  4. Ongoing: Participate in hackathons, help new members, work on advanced projects
  5. Future: Lead workshops, mentor others, contribute to club initiatives
